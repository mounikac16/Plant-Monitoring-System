WEB GIS BASED SOLUTION FOR PLANT WATER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM BASED ON IoT

The Internet plays a major role for internet of things which has been in great demand for the modernisation of many conventional methods. Deployment of IoT technology with the help of sensors and networks is present in almost every field and nowadays optimum sensors have been manufactured for the same. Smart plant water management system is possible with the help of IoT that includes applications in monitoring the flow of water to the plant, measuring the temperature, humidity, rainfall and soil moisture present, and accordingly regulating the water flow. Management of actuators and data analysis through observation from different parameters mentioned above using conventional methods for each user and plant requires extensive human labour and very keen observation. To overcome these factors, IoT which plays a major role in making the management efficient, has been incorporated into this project to manage the parameters smartly.
The plant water management system based on IoT is constructed using techniques involving sensor network, wireless communication network, weather station, plant management information acquisition and remote monitoring data receiving integrated through both software and hardware. Once data is received, it is analysed and water flow is regulated accordingly. The plant location can be viewed online and the user can also add more plants and view their management and progress. Through the well planned and logical execution, all resources are being utilised to their fullest extent. It provides a cost effient solution with methodical management of human labour and smart plant management. Water flow is well-regulated, without any wastage and thus each plant gets appropriate resources according their their own needs.
